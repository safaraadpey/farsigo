# FarsiGo Philosophy

## Why FarsiGo?

FarsiGo was created with one simple question:

**Can Persian become significantly easier without losing its identity?**

We believe the answer is yes.

FarsiGo is an AI-assisted simplified fork of Persian for learning, writing, and communication.

It is not a replacement for Persian.

It is an experiment in making Persian easier while preserving its vocabulary, culture, and spirit.

---

## 1. Simplicity First

Every rule should be as simple as possible.

If two rules can become one, they should become one.

If information is already expressed once, it should not be encoded again in grammar.

Example:

Standard Persian:

دیروز من به مدرسه رفتم.

The word "دیروز" already tells us the sentence is in the past.

So the verb does not need to show past tense again.

FarsiGo:

دیروز من مدرسه رفتن.

One idea.

One rule.

---

## 2. Consistency Over Exceptions

The same idea should follow the same rule.

Exceptions should be minimized.

Example:

Standard Persian has many imperative forms:

برو  
بخور  
بگو  
بنشین  

FarsiGo uses one rule:

مصدر + کن

Examples:

رفتن کن.  
غذا خوردن کن.  
گفتن کن.  
نشستن کن.

For negative commands:

مصدر + نکن

Examples:

رفتن نکن.  
دروغ گفتن نکن.

---

## 3. Persian Identity Preserved

FarsiGo should still feel like Persian.

It keeps Persian vocabulary, word order, rhythm, and cultural identity.

Only unnecessary grammatical complexity is reduced.

Example:

Standard Persian:

من دارم غذا می‌خورم.

FarsiGo:

من می غذا خوردن.

Pinglish:

man mi ghaza khordan.

The sentence is simpler, but it still feels Persian.

---

## Mission

Make Persian easier.

Not smaller.

Not poorer.

Simply easier.

---

**Simple. Consistent. Persian.**
