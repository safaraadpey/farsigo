# Basic Sentences

# Hello, FarsiGo

The examples below compare English, Standard Persian, FarsiGo, and Pinglish.

---

## Greetings

English

Hello.

Standard Persian

سلام.

FarsiGo

سلام.

Pinglish

salam.

---

English

How are you?

Standard Persian

حالت چطور است؟

FarsiGo

حال تو چطور هست؟

Pinglish

hal to chetor hast?

---

English

I am fine.

Standard Persian

من خوب هستم.

FarsiGo

من خوب هست.

Pinglish

man khub hast.
